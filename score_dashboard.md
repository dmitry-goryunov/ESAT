## Interpretation rules

| Pattern | Interpretation | Action |
|---|---|---|
| High accuracy, slow timing | method too heavy | practise elimination and shorter routes |
| Low accuracy, good timing | knowledge or careless errors | review topic, then retest |
| High skipped count | confidence or recognition issue | drill first 30-second route identification |
| Rising careless errors | fatigue or over-speeding | reduce volume and improve review |
| Same topic weak twice | real gap | schedule repair session |
