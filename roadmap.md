# ESAT Roadmap

Start: 19 May 2026  
End: 10 August 2026  
Target exam window: October / November 2026

## Phase overview

| Phase | Weeks | Dates | Goal | Exit gate |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | 1 to 3 | 19 May to 8 Jun | Fluency rebuild and weak-area diagnosis | two short sets above 70 percent within time |
| 2 | 4 to 6 | 9 Jun to 29 Jun | Admissions pacing with NSAA and ENGAA | 40-minute modules completed without timing collapse |
| 3 | 7 to 9 | 30 Jun to 20 Jul | Double and conditional triple simulations | stable accuracy under fatigue |
| 4 | 10 to 12 | 21 Jul to 10 Aug | Optimisation, repair and final rehearsal | stable scores and fewer careless errors |

## Phase 1 - Fluency rebuild

Goals:
- diagnose weak areas
- rebuild algebra fluency
- start short timed sets
- begin mistake logging
- enforce the 60-second skipping rule early

Main sources:
- uploaded MCQ files
- TMUA selected questions
- Isaac Physics repair questions

## Phase 2 - Admissions pacing

Goals:
- move into NSAA and ENGAA questions
- practise 40-minute modules
- develop skipping strategy
- identify recurring traps
- add selected PAT concept repair only where needed

Main sources:
- NSAA Section 1
- ENGAA Section A
- TMUA extracts
- selected PAT questions

## Phase 3 - Simulation and stamina

Goals:
- build fatigue resistance
- practise exam-day pacing
- reduce careless errors
- introduce triple simulations only if double modules are stable

Main sources:
- ENGAA
- NSAA
- ESAT specimens
- mixed custom mocks
- selected PAT repair sets

## Phase 4 - Optimisation

Goals:
- reduce volume slightly
- sharpen technique
- reattempt hardest mistakes
- stabilise scores
- avoid burnout

Main sources:
- error log
- weakest prior papers
- official ESAT material

Avoid new PAT papers unless they fix a specific repeated weakness.

## Readiness gates

| Decision | Evidence required |
|---|---|
| Add more speed pressure | accuracy above 70 percent and low careless errors |
| Add more volume | timing stable and review completed properly |
| Reduce volume | careless errors rising or stamina/fatigue logged twice |
| Add PAT repair | repeated conceptual weakness in Physics |
| Replace planned session | error log shows a more urgent weakness |
