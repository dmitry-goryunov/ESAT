# 12-Week ESAT Preparation Schedule

Start: 19 May 2026  
End: 10 August 2026  
Target exam window: October / November 2026

## Operating rules

- Short aggressive timed sets in Weeks 1 to 3.
- Full 40-minute modules from Week 4 onwards.
- Official ESAT online material starts in Week 6 only.
- Triple simulations are conditional until Week 8.
- PAT is selected concept repair only, never the main timing benchmark.
- Update `error_log.md` and `score_dashboard.md` after every timed session.

## Source rules

| Source | Use | Timing |
|---|---|---|
| Uploaded docs | early topic fluency | 15 Q in 20 to 25 min |
| TMUA | Maths fluency and logic | 15 to 20 Q in 25 to 30 min |
| NSAA | science and maths pacing | 20 to 25 Q in 30 to 40 min |
| ENGAA | admissions-style speed | 20 Q in 30 to 40 min |
| ESAT specimens | official calibration | 27 Q in 40 min |
| ESAT samples | pressure testing | 27 Q in 40 min |
| PAT | concept repair only | untimed or lightly timed selected questions |
